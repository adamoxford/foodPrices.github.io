South Africa's inflation has seen significant shifts since 2021. This chart shows the monthly year-on-year CPI for nine key indicators. By default, the main "All Items" CPI and overall "Food" inflation are highlighted.

In December 2021, South Africa recorded a 5.5% year-on-year increase in food inflation. This was driven by above-inflation rises for Meat and Oils and fats.

As you scroll, the chart highlights these two specific categories and adds a large dot to every line for December 2021.

Scrolling past the specific events, we can again see the overall trend as we return to the initial view.
